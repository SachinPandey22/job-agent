# Job Search Preferences

## Target roles
- Software Engineer Intern
- SWE Intern
- Software Development Engineer Intern (SDEI)
- Backend Engineer Intern
- Full Stack Engineer Intern

## Location
- USA anywhere (all 50 states)
- Remote strongly preferred but open to onsite/hybrid

## Timeline
- Targeting Summer 2025 and Fall 2025 internships
- Available: May 2025 onwards

## Compensation
- Minimum: $25/hour or $50,000 annualized
- Target: $35–45/hour (market rate for SWE intern)

## H1B sponsorship
- Preferred but not required
- Prioritize companies with H1B sponsorship history when possible

## Company preferences
- Prefer: Tech companies, startups with real product, research labs
- Open to: Any industry that uses software engineering
- See blacklist.md for companies to avoid

## Stack preferences (for fit scoring)
- Strong match: Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Django, PostgreSQL, REST APIs
- Good match: Any backend, any cloud platform, any modern frontend
- Weak match: iOS/Android native, embedded systems, COBOL

## Minimum fit score to apply
- 6/10 or above
- 5/10 if company is a strong brand name or known H1B sponsor

## Application limits
- Max 10 applications per day to maintain quality
- Always tailor resume and cover letter — no generic applies
